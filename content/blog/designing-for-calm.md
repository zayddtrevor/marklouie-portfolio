## Calm is not empty

Useful interfaces give people room to think. That usually means fewer visual tricks, clearer hierarchy, and motion that explains where attention should go.

The best dark interfaces still need contrast, rhythm, and restraint. They should feel focused, not hidden.

## What I reach for

- Generous spacing around decisions
- Sharp labels instead of decorative copy
- Motion that confirms a state change
- Mobile layouts that keep the main action close

Calm design is ultimately practical. It helps the product get out of its own way.
