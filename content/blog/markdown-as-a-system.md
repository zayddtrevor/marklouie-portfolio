## Files are a feature

Markdown keeps writing close to the codebase. For a portfolio, that can be enough: front matter for metadata, a slug for routing, and plain text that can move anywhere later.

This site reads posts from `content/blog`, sorts them by date, and renders each file through a small server-side parser.

## Why it works

- The content is versioned with the site
- Drafting does not need a dashboard
- The structure can grow into MDX or a CMS later

Small systems are easiest to trust when their boundaries are visible.
